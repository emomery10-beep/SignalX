# Seat-Based Pricing: The SaaS Model That's Revolutionizing POS

**How Per-Seat Pricing Creates Fairness, Scalability, and Predictable Revenue**

---

## The Problem with Traditional POS Pricing

### Old Model: Flat Monthly Fee
```
Restaurant with 1 cashier:   £49/month
Restaurant with 5 cashiers:  £49/month (Same price!)
Restaurant with 10 cashiers: £49/month (Still same!)
```

**Issues**:
- ❌ Unfair: Big business pays same as small
- ❌ No incentive: More staff shouldn't cost more
- ❌ Unpredictable: Can't budget per employee
- ❌ Barrier to entry: Small business forced to pay enterprise price

### Old Model: % of Revenue Fee
```
Restaurant with £100k monthly sales: £3,000/month (3% cut)
Café with £10k monthly sales:        £300/month (3% cut)
```

**Issues**:
- ❌ Vendor gets percentage of customer's business
- ❌ Disincentivizes growth (more sales = higher cost)
- ❌ Unpredictable costs as business grows
- ❌ Feels like hidden tax on success

---

## The Solution: Seat-Based Pricing

```
Price per staff member per month: £5
Number of cashiers: 3

Monthly Cost: 3 × £5 = £15
```

### Why This Works

✅ **Fair**: You pay for what you use
✅ **Scalable**: Add staff, add cost proportionally
✅ **Predictable**: Know your POS cost exactly
✅ **Inclusive**: All features available at any price point
✅ **Growth-friendly**: Growing business doesn't punish you

## The "Seat" Explained

### What Counts as a Seat?

✅ **Active Staff Members**
- Cashier processing sales
- Inventory manager tracking stock
- Admin viewing reports

❌ **Does NOT Count**
- Owner/manager viewing reports (usually included)
- Inactive staff profiles
- Customers (only staff need login)

### Adding/Removing Seats

**Scenario 1: Seasonal Hiring**
```
September: Hire 2 holiday staff
├─ Add 2 seats
├─ Cost increases by £10/month
└─ Remove seats in January when they leave

Flexible, no long-term commitment
```

**Scenario 2: Permanent Growth**
```
Year 1: 2 staff
Year 2: 3 staff (added cashier)
├─ Add 1 seat
├─ Cost goes from £10 to £15/month
└─ Reflect on your growth
```

## Common Questions

**Q: Does the owner/manager count as a seat?**
A: Typically no. The owner dashboard is included free. You only pay for staff who need login access.

**Q: Can I pause seats during slow seasons?**
A: Yes. Remove staff logins, resume them later. Flexible month-to-month.

**Q: What if I have seasonal peaks?**
A: Add seats in peak season (Dec holidays, summer), remove in low season. No long-term commitment.

**Q: How do add-ons work?**
A: Each account can enable/disable add-ons independently. Enable Payments? +£5/month. Add Tax module? +£5/month.

**Q: Can I downgrade?**
A: Yes. Remove seats, disable add-ons anytime. No cancellation fees.
