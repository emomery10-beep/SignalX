# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

SME founders, solo sellers, and small business owners — globally distributed (UK, Africa, Middle East, South/Southeast Asia, Americas). They run Shopify stores, Amazon FBA operations, physical retail, restaurants, and logistics businesses. They are operators, not analysts: no SQL, no data team, no time for dashboards. They open the app to answer one question and make one decision.

## Product Purpose

AskBiz is an AI business intelligence platform for SME founders. You connect your store (Shopify, Amazon, TikTok Shop, QuickBooks) or upload a CSV, then ask questions in plain English and get specific answers grounded in your actual numbers. It also includes a full Point of Sale system (register, inventory, staff, multi-branch, tax compliance). The goal: replace 2 hours of Monday reports with 4 minutes of direct answers.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, direct, confident. No-nonsense and data-forward. Built for founders who want answers, not dashboards. The tone is that of a blunt, trusted analyst — it never hedges, never over-explains, always gives you the number and the action.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS blue: the navy/blue/white enterprise look (Salesforce, HubSpot energy) — never
- Startup-cream warm: the warm beige/sand/paper AI-generated look saturating 2026 — never
- Dashboard hero-metric template: big number + small label + gradient accent, repeated as decoration — never
- Overly playful: bouncy animations, illustration-heavy, emoji-driven decoration — never

## Design Principles

1. **Answer first, explain second.** Every UI element should feel like it's already holding the answer. No preamble, no setup.
2. **Earn trust with specificity.** Vague claims dissolve trust. Show real numbers, real categories, real consequences. The design should feel like it knows something.
3. **Directness is the aesthetic.** Sharp edges, precise type, no decorative blur or glass that doesn't earn its keep. The visual style IS the product voice.
4. **Global by default, local at the surface.** The product serves 40+ currencies and 6 continents — the design should feel at home anywhere, not London-centric or Silicon Valley-centric.
5. **Speed reads as confidence.** Fast animations, tight copy, decisive layout. Hesitation in the motion = hesitation in the product.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA (4.5:1 body text minimum). Reduced motion alternative required for all animations. RTL layout support exists (Arabic). Color should never be the only signal for status.
