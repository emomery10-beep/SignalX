# AskBiz Facebook Group — Post Bank (Nairobi Sheng)

**Goal:** grow the AskBiz community group. Post **2 per day** (e.g. one morning ~8am, one evening ~7pm — when traders are on their phones).

**Voice:** Nairobi Sheng. Keep business terms in English (*camera, stock, till, scan, sale, free, card, browser, M-Pesa, POS*) — that's how people actually talk. Never translate them into formal Swahili.

**Link every post can end with:** 👉 askbiz.co

**Rotation:** work top to bottom, 2 a day. When you reach the end, loop back — by then the group has new eyes. Swap the emoji/opening line on a repeat so it doesn't feel copy-pasted.
