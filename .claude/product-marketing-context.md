# AskBiz — Product Marketing Context

## Product
**AskBiz** (askbiz.co) — AI business intelligence platform for SME founders.
Connect Shopify, Amazon, TikTok Shop, QuickBooks, WooCommerce, or upload CSVs.
Ask questions in plain English and get answers grounded in your real business data.

## Pricing
- **Free** — 10 questions/month, CSV uploads, Business Pulse score. No card.
- **Growth** — £19/month (3 months free trial). Unlimited questions, Daily Brief, social commerce, churn intelligence, export market scoring.
- **Business** — £39/month (3 months free trial). Everything in Growth + team seats (up to 5), Decision Memory, Competitor Watch, CFO Mode, priority support.
- **Point of Sale add-on** — £5/seat/month. Camera-first barcode scanning, multi-currency (150+), mobile money (M-Pesa, MTN, Airtel), multi-branch, inventory management, staff shifts, tax compliance (VAT, GST, FIRS, KRA, SARS, etc.), Xero/QuickBooks sync.

## Core personas
1. **East Africa founder** — Kenya/Nigeria/Ghana SME owner, uses M-Pesa, sells locally and wants to export. Pain: no affordable BI tool built for Africa.
2. **UK ecommerce founder** — Shopify/WooCommerce seller, £100k–£2M revenue, drowning in spreadsheets. Pain: GA4 doesn't answer business questions.
3. **US small business owner** — QuickBooks user, brick-and-mortar or hybrid. Pain: no clear picture of margins and cash flow.

## Brand voice
- Direct, plain English — no jargon, no fluff
- Warm but confident — like a smart CFO who actually explains things
- Never condescending — founders are smart, they just lack the right tool
- No hype, no "game-changing" — let the product speak

## Competitive landscape
- **Fathom Analytics** — web analytics only, no business questions
- **Tresl / Daasity** — Shopify analytics, expensive, no plain English interface
- **Looker / Tableau** — enterprise BI, requires a data team
- **ChatGPT / Claude directly** — no real data connection, hallucination risk
- **AskBiz moat** — camera-first input, real connected data, SME-priced, multi-market (UK + Africa + US)
