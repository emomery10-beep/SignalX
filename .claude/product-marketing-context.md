# AskBiz — Product Marketing Context

## Product
**AskBiz** (askbiz.co) — phone POS and daily business tracker for market stalls, street vendors, and informal business owners.
Your camera is your till: scan items, take M-Pesa, MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, cash, or card, and see exactly what you made today — on any phone, free to start. No hardware, no spreadsheet, no accountant required.
Also connects to Shopify, Amazon, TikTok Shop, QuickBooks, WooCommerce, Xero, and more for sellers with ecommerce channels.

## Pricing
- **Free** — 10 questions/month, CSV uploads, Business Pulse score. No card.
- **Growth** — £19/month (3 months free trial). Unlimited questions, Daily Brief, social commerce, churn intelligence, export market scoring.
- **Business** — £39/month (3 months free trial). Everything in Growth + team seats (up to 5), Decision Memory, Competitor Watch, CFO Mode, priority support.
- **Point of Sale add-on** — £5/seat/month. Camera-first barcode scanning, multi-currency (150+), mobile money (M-Pesa, MTN, Airtel), multi-branch, inventory management, staff shifts, tax compliance (VAT, GST, FIRS, KRA, SARS, etc.), Xero/QuickBooks sync.

## Core personas
1. **Africa informal seller** — market stall, street vendor, food stand owner in Kenya/Nigeria/Uganda/Ghana. Currently uses a notebook. Pain: no affordable phone-first tool that takes M-Pesa and tracks stock. This is the primary audience.
2. **East Africa SME founder** — Kenya/Nigeria/Ghana small business owner, uses M-Pesa, sells locally and potentially online. Pain: no BI tool built for African markets and currencies.
3. **UK ecommerce founder** — Shopify/WooCommerce seller, £100k–£2M revenue, drowning in spreadsheets. Pain: no clear picture of margins and cash flow.
4. **US small business owner** — QuickBooks user, brick-and-mortar or hybrid. Pain: no clear picture of margins and cash flow.

## Competitive landscape
- **Fathom Analytics** — web analytics only, no business questions
- **Tresl / Daasity** — Shopify analytics, expensive, no plain English interface
- **Looker / Tableau** — enterprise BI, requires a data team
- **ChatGPT / Claude directly** — no real data connection, hallucination risk
- **AskBiz moat** — camera-first input, M-Pesa/mobile money built in, no hardware required, works for unregistered businesses, multi-market (Africa-first + UK + US)
